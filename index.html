EDGI Version Monitoring Bookmarklet

  • Drag this link to your bookmarks bar: EDGI Version Monitoring Bookmarklet.
  • Open the web UI and navigate to a Site View. Click the bookmarklet.
  • The bookmarklet will highlight pages that have changed since your last work session and that have multiple versions.
  • Click the highlighted links to quickly jump to the page comparing the most recent version to the version you last saw.
  • You'll need to enable popups when prompted by your browser.

Do you use this bookmarklet? Do you have an idea to make it better? Share your feedback by creating an issue on GitHub.
